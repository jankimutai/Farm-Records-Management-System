FARM RECORD KEEPING

What is farm record keeping?

Detailed farm records is crucial when making important farming decissions.Farm records includes expenses,labor,harvest and yield records.In this blog, we will discuss the importance of farm record keeping and some tips on how to keep accurate records.

  • Track income and expenditure
  • Improve crop management
  • Better farm planning and forecasting

Tips for keeping accurate farm records.

  • Choose a record-keeping system
  • Better decision making
  • Be consistent: Consistency is key when it comes to record-keeping. Make sure to record everything in the same way every time to avoid confusion and errors.
  • Keep records organized
  • Review records regularly
  • Increases Sales

A Blog on Maize Farming

Maize farming is one of the most important activity in the Rift Valley region. It is a staple food crop that is used for human consumption, animal feed, and industrial purposes. In this blog post, we will provide a comprehensive guide on maize farming, covering everything from planting to harvesting.

1. Planting Maize

It encompases a variety of activities such as land preparation:

  • land clearing
  • tillage
  • soil fertility enhancement
  • Choosing the right variety of maize for your region and soil type. Poor seed quality often leads to low yields and poor grain quality
  • Planting the maize seed at the right depth and spacing
  • soil pH enhancement

    • 2. Maize Care

  • Monitoring the maize plant for pests and diseases
  • Apply topdressing at the right time

    • 3. Harvesting and Storage

  • Harvest maize when fully mature
  • Store the harvested maize in a cool and dry place

    • To preserve maize after harvest, farmers should treat their maize against storage pests such as weevils or use gunny bags. This increases their shelf-life and reduces risk of post harvest loss
